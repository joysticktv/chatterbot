Introducing

ChatterBot

This chat bot will post random messages to your chat when your chat gets quiet. If no one has said anything in a bit, and there's people lurking, it will try to encourage more interactions by saying random funny things, calling people out that are lurking, and sometimes include some helpful tips on how to send tokens, or get social links, etc...

Keep the bot quiet by talking in chat.

  • Send Messages. This bot will have access to send messages in your chat. These messages may change over time.
  • Read Messages. ChatterBot will be able to read all of the chat messages in your live streams.
  • Receive Stream Events. All stream events that happen in your chat will be sent to ChatterBot. This will include things like tips, subscriptions, follows, etc...
  • View User Presence. ChatterBot will know every time a user joins your channel and when they leave.

This is an official chat bot brought to you by the devs behind Joystick.TV.
